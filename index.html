About the Hero

Captain Vikram Batra was a fearless officer of the Indian Army, known for his bravery during the Kargil War (1999). He was posthumously awarded the Param Vir Chakra, India's highest military honor, for his exceptional courage and leadership in recapturing **Point 4875 from enemy forces.

Service Details

  • Born: September 9, 1974
  • Martyrdom: July 7, 1999
  • Rank: Captain
  • Unit: 13 Jammu & Kashmir Rifles
  • Operation: Operation Vijay (Kargil War)
